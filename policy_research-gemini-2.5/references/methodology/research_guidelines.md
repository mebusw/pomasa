# Research Guidelines

## Data Collection
- Prioritize primary sources (government websites) over news aggregators.
- Cross-reference statistics to ensure accuracy.
- When citing, provide the full title and URL if available.

## Analysis Framework
- **PESTEL Analysis**: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, Legal.
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats (for Desay SV).

## Tone and Style
- **Professional**: Use formal language.
- **Objective**: Base opinions on facts.
- **Concise**: Avoid fluff; get to the point.
